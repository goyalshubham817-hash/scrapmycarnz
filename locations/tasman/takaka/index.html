Frequently Asked Questions — Takaka

How much will I get for my scrap car in Takaka?

The value depends on the make, model, year, weight, condition, and current New Zealand scrap metal prices. We regularly pay between $200 and $15,000. Even completely written-off or non-running vehicles receive competitive offers. Submit your rego number for a free, no-obligation quote specific to your vehicle.

Do you offer same-day removal in Takaka?

Yes — in the vast majority of cases we can collect your vehicle the same day you call or submit your quote request. Our drivers operate locally in Takaka and the surrounding area seven days a week, including all public holidays throughout the year.

Does the car need a current WOF or registration?

No. Because the vehicle is being scrapped rather than resold for road use, it does not require a current Warrant of Fitness or registration. We buy vehicles in any condition — running or not, registered or unregistered, damaged, or intact.

Is car removal really free with no hidden charges?

Absolutely. All towing, collection, and transportation costs are covered by us. There are no fees deducted from your payment and no surprise charges on collection day. The price we quote is the exact price you receive — guaranteed.

How do I notify NZTA when scrapping my car?

You are legally required to notify NZTA when a vehicle is scrapped, by completing the MR15 disposal form. This removes the vehicle from your name and protects you from any future fines or liabilities. Our team handles this entire process on your behalf at no additional cost.

Can you buy a car that still has finance owing on it?

Unfortunately not. The outstanding finance must be fully settled before we can purchase the vehicle. Once the loan is cleared, simply contact us and we will arrange same-day pickup at a time that suits you.

What types of vehicles do you accept in Takaka?

We accept all motor vehicles including cars, utes, vans, SUVs, 4WDs, trucks, and campervans of any age or condition. We regularly buy popular New Zealand models including Toyota Hilux, Corolla, and Camry, Ford Ranger and Falcon, Mitsubishi Outlander and Triton, Subaru Forester, Nissan Navara, and many more.
