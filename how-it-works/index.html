What Happens After Collection?

Every vehicle we collect is responsibly processed at a licensed recycling facility. Nothing is wasted and nothing goes to landfill that can be recycled.

Depollution

All hazardous fluids — engine oil, brake fluid, coolant, and refrigerants — are safely extracted and sent to specialist treatment facilities.

Parts Salvage

Engines, gearboxes, wheels, doors, and electronics are removed and resold as quality second-hand parts, reducing the need for new manufacturing.

Precious Metal Recovery

Catalytic converters containing platinum, palladium, and rhodium are carefully recovered by specialist refiners.

Metal Recycling

The vehicle body is crushed, shredded, and sorted. Steel and aluminium are sold to smelters and reformed into new products. Approximately 80% of a vehicle is recyclable.

NZTA Deregistration — Fully Handled for You: We complete the NZTA MR15 vehicle disposal form on your behalf at no extra cost. This legally removes the vehicle from your name. Read our FAQ for full details on legal obligations.

Common Questions About Our Process

How long does the whole process take?

From first contact to cash in hand, same-day collections typically take between 1 and 4 hours. Call us in the morning and your car could be gone before lunch.

Do I need to be home for collection?

Yes. You or an authorised representative must be present to hand over keys, verify identity, and receive payment. We cannot collect without the registered owner or their authorised agent being present.

What documents do I need?

Your vehicle registration number and photo ID (driver's licence or passport). Original papers or WOF certificates are helpful but not required. We can look up all history via NZTA using your rego plate.

Can I reschedule my collection?

Yes. Call us on 0800 000 523 to reschedule. We ask for as much notice as possible but understand plans change. No cancellation fees apply.
